My Wife's Favorite Lasagna Recipe

Steps:

  1. Just go buy it from the store
  2. Preheat the oven to 375F
  3. Make sure to leave the film on the tray; do not vent
  4. Place tray on the middle oven rack. Cook for 45 mins.
  5. Uncover and cook for another 5 mins
  6. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 mins.
  7. Enjoy!
