Best Hamburger Every

Get your summertime grills ready for the best hamburger ever!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, finally chopped
  • 1/2 cup shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
    Preheat your charcoal grill (No gas grills!)
  2. Combine ground beef and all of the ingredients together.
  3. Use your hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.
  4. Cook the patties on the pre-heated grill until no longer pink
    in the middle and the juice runs clear.
    About 4-5 minutes per side.
  5. An instant thermometer inserted into the middle
    should read 165F (74c)
  6. Remove from the grill and let stand for 10 mins.
  7. Enjoy!
